# Buyer Brief

## Purpose
Self-use (primary residence). Planning to move in within 8-12 months.

## Budget
- Range: Rs 70L - Rs 95L (all-inclusive)
- Base price ceiling: Rs 85L
- Hidden costs buffer: Rs 10L (stamp duty, registration, GST, maintenance deposit, parking)
- Loan: Pre-approved for Rs 60L at 8.5% from HDFC

## Target Locations
**City:** Pune, Maharashtra

**Priority areas (in order):**
1. Hinjewadi Phase 1-2 (near IT park, commute <15 min)
2. Baner (lifestyle, restaurants, connectivity)
3. Wakad (value for money, metro upcoming)
4. Keshav Nagar (emerging, near Koregaon Park)

**Acceptable areas:**
- Balewadi, Pashan, Sus Road, Mahalunge

**Avoid:**
- Hadapsar (flooding), Pimpri (industrial feel), PCMC far areas

## Configuration
- 2 BHK (primary) or compact 3 BHK
- Minimum carpet area: 650 sqft
- Floor preference: 3rd-10th floor (no ground floor, no top floor)
- East or north facing preferred

## Must-Haves
- RERA registered
- Builder with minimum 3 completed projects
- Near public transport (bus/metro within 1km)
- 24/7 water supply
- Covered parking (1 car)
- Good internet infrastructure (fiber available)

## Deal-Breakers
- No OC for ready-to-move property
- Active litigation against builder
- Single access road to society
- Within 500m of industrial zone
- Builder has history of >2 year delays
- No clear title chain

## Nice-to-Haves
- Gym and swimming pool
- Close to good schools (planning family)
- Walking distance to grocery/medical
- EV charging provision
- Vastu compliant
